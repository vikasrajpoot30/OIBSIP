Biography

Naval Ravikant is an Indian-American entrepreneur, angel investor, and philosopher, renowned for his profound insights on wealth, happiness, and personal development. Born in 1974 in New Delhi, he moved to the U.S. at age 9. He co-founded AngelList, a platform for startups to raise capital and recruit talent. Naval is a prolific investor, having backed over 100 companies, including Twitter, Uber, and Yammer. His podcast, *Naval*, explores topics on wealth creation and life philosophies. Naval is celebrated for advocating mental freedom, compound interest in learning, and the importance of pursuing what you love. His wisdom has inspired countless entrepreneurs and thinkers worldwide.
